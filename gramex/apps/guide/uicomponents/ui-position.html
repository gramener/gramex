Position

The .pos-* classes position elements absolutely inside a relatively positioned element.

  • .pos-tl positions on the top left
  • .pos-tc positions on the top center
  • .pos-tr positions on the top right
  • .pos-cl positions on the center left
  • .pos-cc positions on the center
  • .pos-cr positions on the center right
  • .pos-bl positions on the bottom left
  • .pos-bc positions on the bottom center
  • .pos-br positions on the bottom right
